Welcome to Naomi's Vegan Meats!


Naomi Vegan Meets is a delivery and catering company delivering a plant-based lifestyle to homes in the Hampton Roads. Naomi's Vegan Meats is owned and maintained by private chef Naomi Ra-El. She has been vegan for 6 years and couldn't be happier! Our staff wants to share the positive health effects of a vegan diet with our customers.
